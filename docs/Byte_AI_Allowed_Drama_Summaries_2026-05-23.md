## Candidate Priority

Foundation candidate:

1. `荒年全村啃树皮，我有系统满仓肉`

Migration candidates:

1. `幸得相遇离婚时` or `撕夜` for revenge / humiliation / relationship conflict.
2. `云渺1：我修仙多年强亿点怎么了` for cultivation / hidden-power boundaries.
3. `家里家外` or `北往` if we want to demonstrate lower-conflict emotional judgment.
